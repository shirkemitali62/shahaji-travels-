Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Shahaji Travels ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website or mobile application.

  • Payment information — processed securely via Cashfree Payments (we do not store card/UPI details)
  • Device information, IP address, and app usage data for analytics and notifications (FCM tokens)
  • Location data only if you grant permission, to show nearby boarding points

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your bus ticket bookings
  • To send booking confirmations, OTPs, and PDF tickets via SMS/WhatsApp/app notification

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Cashfree Payments — to process transactions securely

We implement industry-standard security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption. Payment transactions are processed through PCI DSS compliant gateways. We do not store sensitive payment credentials on our servers.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

At Shahaji Travels, we believe in a fully customer-friendly refund experience. We offer 100% refunds on all eligible cancellations.

1. Cancellation by Customer — 100% Refund

  • Any time before departure: 100% full refund of the ticket amount
  • No-show (failure to board): 100% refund upon request submitted within 24 hours of scheduled departure
  • Refund requests can be made via app ("My Bookings" → Cancel), phone, or email

2. How to Cancel

  • Open the Shahaji Travels app → "My Bookings" → Select booking → "Cancel Ticket"
  • Contact support: +91 90216 94503
  • Email: digubarge123@gmail.com

3. Refund Processing

  • 100% refund guaranteed on all eligible cancellations
  • Refunds processed within 3–5 business days
  • Credited back to original payment method (UPI, card, or wallet)
  • Cash payment bookings refunded via UPI or bank transfer

4. Cancellation by Shahaji Travels

If we cancel a trip due to operational or emergency reasons:

  • You will be notified immediately via SMS, WhatsApp, and app notification
  • 100% full refund processed within 2–3 business days
  • We will attempt to provide an alternative bus wherever possible

5. QR/UPI Pending Bookings

For bookings with QR/UPI payment pending admin verification: if verification is rejected, a 100% refund is processed within 3 business days.

6. Disputes

For any refund disputes, contact us within 30 days of the travel date at digubarge123@gmail.com. All disputes resolved within 7 working days.
